Pixel Art
My favorite thing to do to pass the time!
In fact, all of the art on this site is by me. Even the font!
Pixel art is amazing. Every pixel matters, and even one pixel can have a huge difference.
The retro look it has can change a game from good to great, and there are good pixel art editors everywhere!
I personally use Aseprite, but there are many others to choose from.
